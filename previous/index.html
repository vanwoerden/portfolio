Patients could track health data, their doctors could see all data, set medication schedules, reminders, and daily health goals. An online triage system also helped doctors and their staff triage patient needs, and patients, healthcare professionals, involving loved ones, and patient education. I owned UX and visual design, mostly for the mobile patient apps.
